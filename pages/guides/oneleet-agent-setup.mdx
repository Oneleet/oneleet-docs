# Oneleet Agent Setup (macOS only)

<Callout type="warning">
  The Oneleet Agent is currently in invite-only beta. If you would like to start using it, please contact us at [support@oneleet.com](mailto:support@oneleet.com).
</Callout>

## Add employees to your tenant (Admins only)

1. Go to the [Oneleet Dashboard](https://app.oneleet.com).
2. Click on the `Management` tab and go to `People`.
3. Make sure everyone you want to add is either a tenant member or an employee.
<Callout type="info">
  An employee has access to the [Oneleet Portal](https://portal.oneleet.com), while a tenant member has access to both the [Dashboard](https://app.oneleet.com) as well as the Portal.
</Callout>

## Download the Oneleet Agent

1. Go to the [Oneleet Portal](https://portal.oneleet.com).
2. Click on the `Devices` tab to download the Oneleet Agent. This will download a `Oneleet.zip` archive file.

## Setup the Oneleet Agent on your device

1. Extract the downloaded archive. This will produce a `Oneleet.app` macOS app bundle.
2. Move `Oneleet.app` to `/Applications`.
3. Launch the `Oneleet` app. You should see the Oneleet icon in your macOS menu bar. Click on the icon to login.
4. You will be prompted with a device code authorisation screen. Click on `Confirm` to authorise your device.
5. Login using your email to authenticate. You should receive an email OTP to login.

Once you have logged in, your device will be connected to your tenant on Oneleet.
